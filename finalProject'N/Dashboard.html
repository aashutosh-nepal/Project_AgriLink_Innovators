Bringing Freshness from Farm to You

What We Believe

We believe in produce. Fresh, vibrant, and packed with flavor. Produce like: Bananas. Mangoes. Papayas. Guavas. Pomegranates. Jack-fruit. Pineapples. Lychees. Chikoo (sapodilla). Apples (Kashmir, of course!). Pears. Oranges (Nagpur’s finest). Limes. Grapes. Watermelons. Muskmelons. Jamun. Starfruit. Custard apples. Dragon fruit. And let’s not forget the vegetables: Onions. Tomatoes. Potatoes. Yams. Carrots. Radishes. Cauliflower. Green peas. Bell peppers. Brinjals (a.k.a. eggplant). Bottle gourd. Bitter gourd. Okra (bhindi). Spinach. Fenugreek leaves (methi). Coriander. Mint. Drumsticks. Turmeric. Ginger. Garlic. Oh, and some seasonal treats: Indian gooseberries (amla), pumpkins, zucchinis, and assorted squashes. The beloved leafy greens—amaranth, mustard, sorrel, and more! From classic staples to unique regional finds, we bring you the best of Indian produce, straight from the farm to your home.
